What's Inside

Everything academic, in one place

From the first day of admission to the final semester — every process is covered.

Student Records

Centralized profiles with academic history, parent details, documents, and contact information — always up to date.

Attendance System

Subject-wise attendance marking by faculty, real-time dashboards, and automatic low-attendance alerts.

Exams & Results

Schedule exams, enter marks, auto-calculate GPA, and publish semester results with downloadable grade cards.

Fee Management

Track tuition and exam fees, generate receipts, manage dues, and support multiple payment modes.

Timetable Scheduling

Build and manage class timetables with room allocation, faculty assignment, and conflict detection.

Notices & Circulars

Publish announcements to students, faculty, or specific departments — instantly and reliably.

Assignments

Faculty create and distribute assignments. Students submit online before the deadline — no paperwork.

Secure Role Access

Separate portals for Super Admin, College Admin, HOD, Faculty, and Students — each with the right permissions, nothing more.

Who Uses It

A portal for every role

Each user gets a dedicated dashboard built around their daily responsibilities.

Super Admin

Creates colleges and college-admin entities, and oversees the platform globally.

College Admin

Manages students, staff, departments, fees, subjects, exams, and notices for one college.

HOD

Department oversight, approval workflows, and faculty performance monitoring.

Faculty

Mark attendance, upload assignments, enter marks, and view timetables.

Student

View attendance, results, timetable, assignments, fee status, and notices.

Lab Staff

Manage lab schedules, equipment inventory, and session records.
