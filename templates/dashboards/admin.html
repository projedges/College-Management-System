Use this order when setting up a fresh academic cycle.

1. Add departments

Common Mistakes

Most rework comes from skipping these checks.

  • Missing fee record after student creation
  • Wrong department for faculty or students
  • No HOD assigned to a department
  • Creating exams before subjects are ready
